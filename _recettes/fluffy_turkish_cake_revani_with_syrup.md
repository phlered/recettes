# Fluffy Turkish Cake Revani With Syrup

## Ingrédients


## Préparation

1. 1+1/2 cup sugar
2. 2 cups of water
3. 1/2 ts lemon juice
4. 4 eggs at room temperature
5. 1/2 cup sugar
6. 1 lemon or orange rind or vanilla
7. 1 tbsp vegetable oil
8. 1/2 cup semolina flour
9. 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
10. 1 package baking powder (2 tsp)
11. about 1/3 cup unsweetened grated coconut
12. Stir water and sugar in a small pan and heat. After boiling for 5 minutes pour dropped lemon juice boil 30 seconds more. Turn off the heat and let it cool.
13. In a big bowl mix eggs, sugar, and lemon rind until light and fluffy about 4 minutes.
14. Add oil, then semolina, flour, and baking powder. Mix on low until everything is corporated.
15. Grease 20 x 28 cm or similar size pan. Pour the cake mixture over and bake in a preheated 170C degree oven until golden brown and risen.
16. After 5 minutes taking out from the oven pour the cooled syrup slowly and wait until it soaks all of it.
17. Sprinkle unsweetened grated coconut generously and serve
